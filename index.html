ABOUT US

WE SEEK OUT A CHALLENGE, SET OUR SIGHTS HIGH & SHOOT FOR THE STARS

Genesys is on of Nigeria's leading game development companies and a wholly-owned subsidiary of Ocagon. We are a new company, we started in 2020 and have published the boundaries of technical and artistic excellence in our games ever since.

Today, we employ more than 350 professionals from 24 different countries. Our studio is located in the heart of Nigeria, Lagos- A great place to work and play.
